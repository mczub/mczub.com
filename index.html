Hi.

My name is Matthew Czubakowski, and welcome to my personal website.

I'm an electrical engineer living in Southern California. I graduated from Caltech in 2009.

By day, I work in the Aerospace industry. I have a broad skillset with experience in mixed-signal ASIC design, systems engineering, software development, and circuit design and test.

By night, I experiment with responsive web design, software development, music, and video game livestreaming.

Feel free to connect with me through social media. I'm easiest to get a hold of through e-mail, Twitter, and Facebook.

Thanks for visiting!
